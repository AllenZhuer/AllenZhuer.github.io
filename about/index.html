About me

Hi, there. This is Allen.

I want to work on products that customers love—products that inspire and provide real value. Have a passion for bridging the gap between technology and the market to solve real customer problems in a way that meets the needs of the business.

This blog will contain what I learned in this path. Now, I have just completed my master’s degree in Management of Technology at the National University of Singapore. At the same time, taking some courses related to Product Management and AI, wishing to make progress. As a tech tracker, knowing the tech trend and the market is essential.

This is the road I choose.

Currently, I see great opportunities in combining AIGC with robotics to perform or replace humans in more highly repetitive tasks. I would like to pursue a PhD in this field to develop my skills and advance the technology, and even turn my research into truly inspired innovation.

Volunteer

I’m glad to share that I’m now a volunteer as a student mentor in Enterprise Development, a module in the Masters of Management of Technology at the National University of Singapore. Bridging students and classes to explore new market opportunities for innovative technologies.

Some introduction about this module: Enterprises need to keep innovating to stay competitive – this can be through new applications for existing technologies, or through the discovery and realization of new products/services for new markets. The process of identifying and qualifying opportunities within enterprises requires an understanding of the business as well as external market forces to result in value creation. This course helps students to experience the innovation process in large corporates.
